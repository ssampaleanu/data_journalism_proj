The Man's Got Us Down


The positive relationship of binge drinking and high employment

This graph demonstrates an unexpected relationship between 2014 binge drinking and high employment.
Employment data was provided by American FactFinder. Binge drinking data was provided by the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System, and is defined by adults who consume 5 or more alcoholic beverages when drinking (4 for women).
